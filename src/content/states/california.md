## Can you take traffic school online in California?

Many California courts may allow eligible drivers to complete traffic school online, but online enrollment is not automatic for every ticket. Your court, citation type, completion deadline, prior traffic school history, and license status can all matter.

Before paying for any course, confirm that your court allows online traffic school for your case and that you understand the deadline for completion.

## Who is usually eligible for traffic school?

Eligibility is usually tied to the type of violation, whether the ticket is eligible, how recently you attended traffic school, and whether the court grants permission. Commercial license situations and more serious violations can have different rules.

Treat eligibility as something to verify with your court, not something to assume from a provider website or referral page.

## How much does California traffic school cost?

Course prices can vary by provider and may not include separate court, certificate, processing, or expedited delivery fees. This page does not claim a cheapest option or publish unverified coupon codes.

Check the provider price before enrolling and compare that with any court fees or administrative costs listed by your court.

## How long does online traffic school take?

Online course timing can depend on provider requirements, identity checks, quizzes, reading time, and your own schedule. California traffic school also has a court deadline, so the safer approach is to start early enough for completion reporting or certificate handling.

## What should you check before enrolling?

Before you leave this guide, verify whether your court allows online traffic school for your citation, your due date, whether the provider reports completion or you submit proof, and the full price including certificate or processing fees.
